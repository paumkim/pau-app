# Pau Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 14, 2026

## What Pau does
Pau is a translation and language assistant app for Zomi speakers. It translates text between Zomi, English, Malay, and Chinese.

## Data we collect
**Pau does not collect any personal data.** Full stop.

- Translation text is sent to Hugging Face's inference API or our own model server to generate translations. We do not log, store, or share your translations.
- No account is required. No tracking. No analytics. No ads.
- The app works entirely on-device when the offline model is downloaded.

## Data storage
- Translation history is stored **only on your device** in local storage. You can clear it anytime.
- If you download the offline model, it stays on your device. You can delete it anytime.

## Internet permission
We need internet to:
1. Send translation requests to the model server (when not in offline mode)
2. Download the offline model file

## Microphone permission
We need microphone access only if you use voice input. Audio is processed on-device or sent to the translation server — it is never stored or shared.

## Third-party services
- **Hugging Face Inference API** — used for cloud translation when the app is in online mode. Their privacy policy applies to data sent through their API.
- **GitHub** — we link to our open-source repository for transparency. No data is sent to GitHub by the app.

## Children's privacy
Pau does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes
If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. No other notice will be given because we literally have no way to contact you — we don't collect your email or any identifier.

## Contact
For questions: pau.app@zomi.dev
